Message to Future Students

What I've learned

  Hey, while some of the assignments may seem like busywork, they were all designed to make us more competitive in the job market nad better prepared for future careers

  Through the course of the class, we learned to develop a polished resume, hone our networking skills, make a linkedin page (which is suprisingly important), and practice interviewing techniques.

  Doing this stuff made a big difference for me because to be honest I wouldn't be practicing or doing this stuff in school outside the context of a class. Being here helped me hunker down and focus on getting this done making the transition to a career after college much easier.

  One thing that has stuck with me and I'd like to share with you is that career readiness doesn't have to mean sacrificing your individuality for the sake of a corporation. Instead, we were encouraged to identify our goals and values and find a career path that aligned with them. You don't have to become Allen Bateman to have a nice life, and your work and what you want to experience in life can have quite a bit of overlap
